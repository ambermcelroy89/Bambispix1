About Me

Hello everyone. Bambi’s the name and photography is my game. In life we are blessed many times over, so why not capture the important moments? Snapshots in time provide comfort and joy for years to come and I’m the girl to get yours captured perfectly. I do offer small event specials, portrait photography, and nature moments. Peek in on my portfolio and message me with how we can make beautiful memories together.

I specialize in taking photos of young kids and pets, but am no stranger to large event and family portraits.

~ Amber “Bambi” McElroy
